# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a respectful and harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Use welcoming and inclusive language.
- Respect differing viewpoints and experience levels.
- Give and receive constructive feedback.
- Focus on what is best for the project and its users.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks.
- Public or private abuse.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Repeated disruption of project discussions.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, issues, pull requests, or other contributions that do not align with this Code of Conduct. Maintainers may also temporarily or permanently restrict participation for behavior that harms the project community.

## Reporting

If you need to report a concern, open a private security advisory when appropriate or contact the maintainer through GitHub.
